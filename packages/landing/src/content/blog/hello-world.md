We're excited to introduce Lobu — an open-source platform that deploys sandboxed OpenClaw agents on demand for every user and channel.

## Why Lobu?

Teams need AI agents that are secure, isolated, and easy to manage. Lobu gives you:

- **Sandboxed execution** — every agent runs in its own container with no direct internet access
- **Your own keys** — bring your own provider credentials, nothing leaves your infrastructure
- **Multi-platform** — works with Telegram, Slack, and WhatsApp out of the box
